How SeaKeepers As A Non-Profit Encourage Community Engagement

Expand Reach and Awareness

Non-profits like the SeaKeepers who's primary focus is ocean conservation boost the knowledge to the importance of the topic to those who collaborate with them, whether it be through volunteers or partnerships. They build Awareness for the issue of climate change and the things it effects in the ocean through their continuous efforts.

Access and Impact

The SeaKeepers have many programs that push for people to get involved with ocean conservation. Through there programs they allow access to places and areas most wouldnt be able to go or even know about. One of their most accessible programs would be their volunteer initiatves, anyone can access and participate in their weekly beach clean-ups to have contribute to a cleaner community.
